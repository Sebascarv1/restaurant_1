Who we are

We are a family-run business founded from the heartfelt desire to bring the vibrant flavors of Colombia and Belgium together. Born from the necessity of sharing the colorful spices and rich culinary heritage of Latin America, combined with the comforting traditions of Belgian cuisine, we’ve created a unique blend that delights taste buds across Europe.

Our journey began in the streets of Gent, inspired by our family’s love for food and community, and the belief that every dish tells a story. By combining the zest and energy of Colombian street food with the warm and wholesome flavors of Belgium, we’ve set out to bridge two worlds and offer a taste of home to every visitor.

At Food Truck Restaurant, every meal is made with love, using authentic ingredients and recipes handed down through generations. Join us as we invite you to taste the magic of a multicultural journey on a plate!
